Our Story

Ourmissionisto accompany architectsand interior designers

Since our creation in 2010, José Luis Latrás, founder of DINGQI, laid the foundations of a company focused on quality and service, where people are the centre of our philosophy.

The ability to adapt and the commitment of a great team made that small shop in Archena Street in Zhongshan become an internationally recognised firm with a presence in more than 90 countries.

Our experience over 40 years has allowed us to develop our own language that combines sophistication and technological innovation. Understanding light and knowing how to use it in our own unique way is essential to create atmospheres and sensations that improve people's lives.

2010

Founded in Zhongshan

José Luis Latrás opens the first DINGQI office on Archena Street, Zhongshan — focused on quality technical lighting.

2000s

International Expansion

DINGQI begins its international journey, reaching distributors across Europe and beyond.

2014

First Red Dot Award

Lark-111 wins the prestigious Red Dot Design Award — the first of 15 to follow.

2020

Minimal Track Launch

The Minimal Track 24V system wins two Red Dot Awards in the same year, becoming a landmark product.

2024

Carbon Neutral Certification

DINGQI achieves Carbon Neutral certification — a milestone in our sustainability journey.

2026

The 2026 Collection

Launch of the 2026 catalogue featuring Nano, Zenit, Enso, Divi and more innovative luminaires.

What Drives Us

Our Values

01

Innovation

Innovation is at the heart of our philosophy. We are committed to delivering advanced lighting solutions that not only transform spaces, but also elevate our customers' experience. We constantly seek new ways to create to ensure efficiency and exceptional design.

02

Accompaniment

In our mission to accompany architecture and design professionals around the world, DINGQI dialogues with and learns from them to provide the lighting tools that best suit their needs.

03

Quality

We work for continuous improvement of our luminaires. With meticulous precision, the final product is the result of the entire work process we carry out. Design, material and versatility bring our luminaires to life.

Sustainability

More Light,
Less Carbon

Lighting is not just a source of light; it is an opportunity to create environments that respect our planet. Our work is moving towards a more sustainable future, where lighting technology embraces the highest standards of performance.

From the use of state-of-the-art LED technologies to smart design and the integration of recyclable materials, every detail has been carefully considered to boost energy efficiency and minimise environmental impact.
