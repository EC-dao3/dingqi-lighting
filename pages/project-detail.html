Dentop Clinic

Dentop Clinic is a project by Hype Project that transforms the clinical space into a calm and welcoming experience. Through carefully considered architecture, warm materials and soft yet precise lighting, the clinic prioritises patient wellbeing while supporting the functional needs of the medical team.

Dentop redefines the dental experience through a serene and carefully designed environment. From the very first moment, the space developed by Hype Project conveys a sense of calm through a neutral beige colour palette combined with soft textures and pleasant finishes. Everything is conceived to help patients feel comfortable and supported, moving away from the traditional clinical perception.

Upholstered furniture with enveloping forms encourages moments of pause, while warm wood tones and soft textiles bring visual comfort and a welcoming character. The lighting, gentle in nature, creates a relaxed and inviting atmosphere. At the same time, it ensures excellent lighting quality in work areas, where precision and visual comfort are essential for the medical team.

Beyond its refined aesthetic language, the project balances wellbeing and functionality. Circulation areas, waiting spaces, and treatment rooms are designed to enhance both the patient experience and the operational efficiency of healthcare professionals. Each spatial decision contributes to a sense of trust, calm and care.

This project, developed in collaboration with Design Radar, demonstrates how architecture and light can transform a healthcare space into a calm environment, accompanying the patient from beginning to end.
